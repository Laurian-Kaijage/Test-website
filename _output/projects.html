Featured Projects

Coastal Biodiversity Monitoring Programme

Status: Ongoing Organisation: Mwambao Coastal Community Network

A long-running ecological monitoring programme tracking reef fish, coral, seagrass, and mangrove health across 7+ sites along Tanzania’s coast. Monitoring data drives annual conservation reporting and informs government marine spatial planning.

What I do: - Design and lead biodiversity survey protocols - Coordinate and train field teams and community monitors - Analyse data using R — trend analysis, species richness, benthic cover - Produce annual reports for government and NGO stakeholders

Outcomes: - Multi-year baseline datasets for 7+ coastal sites - Community-owned monitoring protocols now in use - Data contributed to national MPA planning

Tools: R · QGIS · KoboToolbox · Excel · ggplot2

Coastal GIS & Habitat Mapping

Status: Ongoing Organisation: Mwambao Coastal Community Network

Spatial mapping of critical coastal habitats — seagrass beds, mangrove forests, and coral reefs — using remote sensing data and GPS-verified field surveys. Outputs are used in MPA boundary proposals and management plans.

What I do: - Process and classify satellite imagery for habitat extent mapping - Ground-truth habitat boundaries with field surveys - Create publication-quality maps and spatial reports

Outcomes: - Detailed habitat extent maps for key conservation sites - Spatial data integrated into government planning documents - Improved understanding of habitat connectivity

Tools: QGIS · ArcGIS · R (sf, terra) · Google Earth Engine

Marine Species Database

Status: Active Development Organisation: Mwambao Coastal Community Network

Building and managing a structured database to compile species observations, monitoring records, and occurrence data from all field programmes. Designed to support long-term trend analysis and regional data sharing.

What I do: - Design database schema for species and site data - Build data pipelines to ingest KoboToolbox field data - Automate quality checks and reporting with R scripts

Outcomes: - Centralised species records from 5+ years of monitoring - Standardised data entry reducing field data errors - Automated monthly summary reports generated

Tools: SQL · R · KoboToolbox · PostgreSQL

Community-Based Conservation Training

Status: Ongoing Organisation: Mwambao Coastal Community Network + Local Partners

Designing and facilitating training programmes for fishing communities, enabling them to conduct their own biodiversity monitoring. Builds local conservation ownership and generates community-level data.

What I do: - Design training curricula for non-scientific audiences - Facilitate hands-on reef survey training sessions - Mentor community monitors over extended periods

Outcomes: - 12+ trained community data collectors active across coastal sites - Community-managed monitoring sustained beyond project cycles - Local conservation knowledge documented and integrated

Tools: KoboToolbox · Training materials · Field guides

Climate Resilience Assessment

Status: Planning Phase Organisation: TBD / In Development

Evaluating how climate change — ocean warming, acidification, sea level rise — is affecting coastal ecosystem resilience at key monitoring sites. Will integrate historical monitoring data with climate projections.

What I do: - Review existing monitoring data for climate-related signals - Develop climate vulnerability scoring for habitats and communities - Model future species and habitat distribution scenarios

Planned Outcomes: - Site-level climate vulnerability assessments - Adaptive management recommendations - Input into regional climate resilience strategies

Tools: R · Species distribution modelling · Climate datasets
